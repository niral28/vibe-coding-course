Methodology & Data Sources

This analysis combines Anthropic's Economic Index data from over 4 million Claude.ai conversations mapped to O*NET occupational tasks, current LLM capability assessments, and job automation potential studies. The Pareto curve identifies jobs that offer the best automation potential relative to current LLM capabilities, helping predict which roles may see the most significant transformation.

LLM Model Positioning: Models positioned based on 2025 benchmark performance and capabilities. Next-gen models (Claude 4, GPT-4.1, o3, Grok-3) show breakthrough capabilities approaching theoretical limits. Chinese models like DeepSeek-V3 and Qwen2.5-Max demonstrate competitive open-source alternatives.
