AI ERA
~300,000 BCE

Hunter-Gatherers

Homo sapiens emerge. Small nomadic tribes survive by hunting megafauna and gathering resources across vast territories.

~10,000 BCE

Agricultural Revolution

Humans domesticate crops and animals. Permanent settlements emerge, enabling population growth and civilization.

~3500 BCE

The Wheel

Revolutionary circular technology transforms transportation, pottery, and mechanical engineering forever.

1440 CE

Printing Press

Gutenberg's movable type democratizes knowledge, accelerating the spread of ideas across continents.

1769

Steam Engine

Watt's improved steam engine unleashes mechanical power, triggering the Industrial Revolution.

1860s

Agricultural Society Peak

80% of Americans work as farmers during the Civil War era. Manual labor dominates human productivity.

1879

Electric Light

Edison's incandescent bulb illuminates the world, extending productive hours and transforming society.

1876

Telephone

Bell's electromagnetic communication device shrinks global distances instantaneously.

1913

Assembly Line

Ford's moving production line increases manufacturing efficiency by 8x, enabling mass production.

1946

ENIAC Computer

First general-purpose electronic digital computer. 30 tons of vacuum tubes process 5,000 operations/second.

1947

Transistor

Bell Labs' semiconductor revolution enables miniaturization of all electronic devices.

1957

Perceptron

Rosenblatt's first artificial neural network algorithm mimics biological brain learning.

1969

ARPANET

First internet message sent between UCLA and Stanford. Digital communication network begins.

1981

IBM Personal Computer

Computing power reaches homes and offices. Digital literacy becomes essential.

1986

Backpropagation

Algorithm enables training of deep neural networks by propagating errors backward.

1991

World Wide Web

Berners-Lee creates the web protocol. Global information sharing becomes accessible to all.

1998

LeNet (CNNs)

LeCun's convolutional neural networks achieve breakthrough in computer vision tasks.

2006

Deep Learning Renaissance

Hinton's breakthrough in training deep networks sparks the modern AI revolution.

2012

AlexNet Victory

Deep CNN wins ImageNet competition by massive margin. Computer vision breakthrough.

2014

Attention Mechanism

Bahdanau attention improves machine translation by focusing on relevant input parts.

2017

Transformer Architecture

"Attention is All You Need" - Google's transformer enables modern large language models.

2018

GPT-1 Launch

OpenAI's first Generative Pre-trained Transformer demonstrates unsupervised language learning.

2020

GPT-3 Emergence

175 billion parameters achieve human-like text generation. AI capabilities explode exponentially.

2022

ChatGPT Launch

AI assistant reaches 100M users in 2 months. Conversational AI enters mainstream consciousness.

2025

AI Singularity Approaching

We stand at the precipice of artificial general intelligence. The future of humanity transforms.
